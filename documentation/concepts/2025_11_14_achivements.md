### Explorer Achievement:
- "Cartographer" - Discover all mini-game points
- "Scout" - First in your faction to visit enemy territory
- "Pathfinder" - Visit all floors

### Strategic Features:
- **Supply Lines** - Show paths between your faction's points
- **Contested Zone Warning** - Highlight areas under attack
- **Rally Point** - Players can mark strategic targets for faction
- **History Replay** - Scrub through time to see territorial changes

### Social Features:
- Pin messages to map locations
- Draw temporary routes/plans for faction
- "Last seen here" for faction members
